    Yellowstone National Park

    Yellowstone National Park is a huge area of land set aside for nature to prosper. Check out the different things Yellowstone has to offer. From its rich history to its hiking options and wildlife.

    Old Faithful

    Yellowstone was the first national park to be founded in the United States. On March 1st, 1872, president Ulysses S. Grant and the U.S. congress signed it into law!

    Grand Canyon of the Yellowstone

    Yellowstone is one of the largest temperate-zone ecosystems on the planet! Sitting at almost 3,500 square miles its home over 100 different species. In fact, 67 different species are mammals, making Yellowstone the largest concentration of mammals in the mainland 48 states!

    Roosevelt Arch

    Located in the northwest corner of Wyoming it sits secluded with entrances from Idaho, Montana, and its southern neighbor Grand Teton National Park! If you ever are looking for a new vacation idea, visit Yellowstone!
